# Feature Ideas Backlog

Raw ideas — not prioritized, not committed. Captured so they don't get lost.

## Check-in Experience
- Voice memo check-ins (transcribed to text)
- Photo attachments (post-run photo, meal prep pic)
- Rotating weekly prompts to keep it fresh
- "Quick" mode — thumbs up/down per F instead of writing
- AI-generated monthly summary of your F progress

## Social / Accountability
- Weekly house score (% of Fs completed by the whole house)
- Scheduled group call triggered when everyone checks in
- "Challenge" mode — set a shared F goal for the week
- Anonymous reactions / spicy takes

## Events & IRL
- Run route suggestions based on member locations
- Strava / Apple Health integration for Fitness F auto-fill
- Shared calendar view of upcoming crew events
- Restaurant suggestions for Fun F meetups

## Retention
- "Your streak is on the line" late Sunday nudge
- Monthly recap email — best F moments
- Crew anniversary reminders
- Year-in-review

## Monetization Ideas
- Free: 1 household (up to 6 members), basic feed
- Pro ($4/mo): unlimited households, streaks, analytics
- Crew ($8/mo/group): event planning, cross-household feed, custom Fs
