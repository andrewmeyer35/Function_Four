# Product Vision

## One-liner
A lightweight accountability app that helps roommates and friend groups stay aligned on what matters most — money, health, social life, and relationships.

## The Four Fs

| F | Full Name | Weekly Prompt |
|---|-----------|---------------|
| 💰 | Financial | What did you do this week to set up long-term financial success? |
| 💪 | Fitness | What are you doing to hit your fitness goals? |
| 🎉 | Fun / Friends | What are you planning to meet your social needs? |
| ❤️ | Flirt / Fervier | What are you doing to meet new people or keep your relationship fresh? |

## Core Loop
1. Every week, each user submits a short check-in (a few sentences per F)
2. Your household sees each other's updates in a shared feed
3. React with emoji, leave encouragement, hold each other accountable
4. Over time, expand to multi-household "crews" across the city

## North Star Metric
**Weekly Active Submitters** — the % of users who complete a 4 F check-in each week.

## Phase Milestones
- **Phase 0** — Manual experiment. Prove the habit loop before writing code.
- **Phase 1** — Web MVP. One household. Core check-in + feed + reminders.
- **Phase 2** — Multi-household. Crews, event planning, streaks.
- **Phase 3** — iOS app. Push notifications, home screen widget, monetization.
